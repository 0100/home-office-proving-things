About Proving Things to Government

We've started to research and design how we can reduce the load on both customers and caseworkers by using data we already have in Government.

We're also developing rules based API's which unlock the ability for other services to use our work.

Here is a collection of prototypes we're testing based on those API's. They're helping to inform our direction and test our work.

Current prototypes

  • Residency - Proving you're in or out of the country
  • Income - Proving you're earning and/or above a determined threshold
